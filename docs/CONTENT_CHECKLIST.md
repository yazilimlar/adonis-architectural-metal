# Client Content Checklist

## Business information
- Legal company name
- Public brand name confirmation
- Address and service territory
- Phone and business email
- Company history
- Team biographies
- Verified capabilities and equipment
- Delivery and installation regions

## Product information
For each product: name, category, dimensions, materials, finishes, customization, lead time, price policy, technical drawing and photographs.

## Project information
Project type, location, scope, design intent, materials, finish, completion date, installation details, client permission and photography.

## Brand assets
Logo source files, preferred marks, colors, existing signage, workshop photography, certifications and social profiles.

## Legal and operations
Privacy contact, terms, warranty policy, shipping/delivery policy, return policy for standard products, and consent for testimonials and photography.
