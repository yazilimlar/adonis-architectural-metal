# Project Charter

## Product
Adonis Architectural Metal public website, portfolio, custom-design inquiry system and marketing foundation.

## Positioning
A design-led architectural metalwork and furniture manufacturer creating custom, high-quality artistic products for residential, hospitality, office and commercial environments.

## Primary audiences
- Homeowners and collectors
- Architects and interior designers
- Hotels and restaurants
- Office and retail clients
- Contractors and developers

## Phase 1 objective
Launch a credible, visually distinctive public site that demonstrates capabilities, organizes products and generates qualified custom-project inquiries.

## Non-negotiables
- Client-owned and isolated technical infrastructure
- Mobile-first responsive experience
- Fast image delivery
- Accessible typography and contrast
- No unverified company claims
- Structured product and project data
- Clear quotation workflow
